I think a lot of things make up good design, but I think one of the key points is attraction. I often think "How can I manipulate someone to look at this?" Design is very subjective, so I don't know if I could say what good design is without knowing who my audience is. I know what I like, but if someone doesn't like what I like, that doesn't make it bad. Also, sometimes something is so bad it's good and vice versa.
